Pizza

Description

Pizza is a beloved Italian-American dish featuring a crispy or chewy crust topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings. Whether you prefer a classic Margherita or a loaded specialty pizza, this versatile dish is perfect for any occasion.

Homemade pizza allows you to customize every element to your taste, from the dough texture to your favorite toppings. The combination of fresh ingredients and a hot oven creates an irresistibly delicious result that beats delivery every time.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet instant yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup pepperoni slices
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 1 cup fresh mushrooms, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves

Steps

  1. Mix flour, yeast, salt, and sugar in a large bowl.
  2. Add olive oil and warm water, mixing until a dough forms.
  3. Knead dough for 8-10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  4. Place dough in an oiled bowl, cover, and let rise for 1 hour until doubled.
  5. Preheat oven to 450°F (230°C).
  6. Punch down dough and stretch it onto a pizza pan or baking sheet.
  7. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a border for the crust.
  8. Sprinkle half of the mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  9. Add desired toppings: pepperoni, bell pepper, onion, and mushrooms.
  10. Top with remaining mozzarella cheese.
  11. Bake for 12-15 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  12. Remove from oven and garnish with fresh basil.
  13. Let cool for 2-3 minutes, slice, and serve.
