Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian pasta dish made with layers of pasta sheets, rich meat sauce, and creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese. This comfort food favorite is perfect for family dinners and special occasions, combining the best flavors of Italy in every bite.

This traditional recipe features tender pasta layers alternating with a savory Bolognese meat sauce and smooth cheese filling, all baked until golden and bubbly. The result is a hearty, satisfying dish that never fails to impress.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 pound ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1 egg
  • 1 box lasagna noodles
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions, drain and set aside.
  3. Heat olive oil in a large pot and sauté diced onion until softened.
  4. Add minced garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  5. Add ground beef, breaking it up with a spoon, and cook until browned.
  6. Stir in tomato paste and cook for 2 minutes.
  7. Add crushed tomatoes, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  8. In a bowl, mix ricotta, 1 cup mozzarella, parmesan, and egg until well combined.
  9. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  10. Layer noodles, then ricotta mixture, then meat sauce. Repeat layers, ending with sauce.
  11. Top with remaining mozzarella cheese.
  12. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  13. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes until cheese is bubbly and golden.
  14. Let rest for 10 minutes before serving.
